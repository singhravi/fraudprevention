01. Social Engineering & Profiles
What defines Social Engineering?
Manipulating human behavior to bypass security systems.
The 'Fake Profile' bait?
Using stolen photos of attractive people or friends to gain trust.
Smishing definition?
Phishing specifically delivered via SMS or Instant Messaging.
Vishing core tactic?
Using voice calls to create panic and steal bank credentials.
What is 'Authority' manipulation?
Posing as CEO or Police to bypass a victim's critical thinking.
How to spot a fake URL?
Check for slight misspellings (e.g., 'g00gle.com' vs 'google.com').
What is 'Pretexting'?
Building a fake story to convince a victim to reveal information.
The 'Baiting' scam?
Promising a reward (free download/gift) to infect a device with malware.
02. Transactions & Hardware
Marketplace Scam Rule?
Never pay 'Security Deposits' for items you haven't seen in person.
ATM 'Shoulder Surfing'?
Physically watching a victim enter their PIN. Cover the keypad!
QR Code Red Flag?
Scanning a code should only be done to PAY, never to RECEIVE money.
SIM Swap Warning Sign?
Sudden loss of network on your phone for no environmental reason.
OTP Management?
Banks will NEVER call to ask for your One-Time Password.
ATM Card Skimming?
Illegal devices on card slots that read your magnetic stripe.
Contactless (NFC) Fraud?
Scammers using portable PoS machines near your wallet. Use RFID-blockers.
Fake Payment Screenshots?
Verify money in your bank app; never trust a 'Successful' screenshot.
03. High-Value Schemes
Ponzi Scheme Red Flag?
Guaranteed 100% returns with 'no risk'. If it's too good to be true, it is.
Instant Loan App Trap?
Asking for access to contacts/photos. Used for blackmail later.
Matrimonial 'Emergency'?
Matches asking for money due to sudden illness or customs delay.
Fake Cashback Offers?
Links claiming you won a reward that lead to a fake UPI PIN page.
Work From Home Scam?
Asking for a 'Kit Fee' or 'Registration Fee' before starting a job.
Investment 'Pig Butchering'?
Long-term friendship built to lure you into a fake crypto platform.
Lottery/KBC Fraud?
Being told you won a contest you never actually participated in.
Deepfake Scams?
AI-generated video or voice of your boss/relative asking for money.
04. Tools, Tech & Reporting
Screen Sharing Danger?
Apps like AnyDesk allow scammers to see your OTPs live.
Fake Customer Care?
Fake numbers listed on Google Maps for banks or airlines.
Immediate Financial Help?
Dial 1930 immediately to freeze transactions in India.
